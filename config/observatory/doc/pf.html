This Guidebook shows realtime analyses of P. falciparum samples from study sites in countries worldwide, providing insight into the distribution and epidemiology of malaria.

Once you're in the Guidebook sections below you can get more a in-depth and interactive look at these data by switching to the Viewer.

All of the genome data used in the Malaria Observatory is open access. For details on how to download the data for your own analyses, see the Learn more section below.
