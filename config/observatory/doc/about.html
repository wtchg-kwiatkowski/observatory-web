What is the MalariaGEN Observatory? The Observatory has three main components.
  1. Open access population genomic data on malaria parasites and vectors
  2. A set of metrics derived from these data that are informative about biology and epidemiology, both for populations and for individual samples
  3. A web application Malariometrics that translates this into useful information for malaria control and elimination
What are Malariometrics? Malariometric indices traditionally include measurements like prevalence of malaria parasitaemia, spleen rate, and anaemia in defined risk groups. The purpose of this webapp is to provide measurements on parasites based on their genomes. For each individual parasite we will generate a number of different metrics, from presence/absence of particular alleles through to haplogroup assignation. Malariometrics presents these metrics in an intuitive and informative way by aggregating information across sites, countries, and regions. Who contributes samples and data to Malariometric?
