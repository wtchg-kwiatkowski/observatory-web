What is the MalariaGEN Observatory? There are three main components that contribute to the MalariaGEN Observatory: Open access population genomic dataset on malaria parasites and vectors A set of metrics derived from these data that are informative about biology and epidemiology The MalariaGEN Analytics web application that translates these metrics into information for malaria control and eliminations What are MalariaGEN Analytics? For each individual parasite we generate a number of different metrics, from presence/absence of particular alleles to, in the case of drug resistance muations, our assignment of a specific lineage, or independent origin, to each alleles. MalariaGEN Analytics presents these metrics in an intuitive and informative way by aggregating information across sites, countries, and regions. Who contributes samples and data to Malariometric? The current data has been contributed by the MalariaGEN network and forms the basis of the latest Pf6 release. Where can I find out more?
