What is the MalariaGEN Observatory? The MalariaGEN Observatory is the new platform being built to bring together genomics datasets and novel analytics for malaria control. It is based on three core pillars: Open access population genomic dataset on malaria parasites and vectors A set of metrics and analyses derived from these data that are informative about biology and epidemiology Web applications that translates these metrics into actionable information for malaria control and elimination What are MalariaGEN Analytics? For each individual parasite we generate a number of different metrics, from presence/absence of particular alleles to, in the case of drug resistance muations, our assignment of a specific lineage, or independent origin, to each alleles. MalariaGEN Analytics presents these metrics in an intuitive and informative way by aggregating information across sites, countries, and regions. Who contributes samples and data to MalariaGEN Analytics? The current data has been contributed by the MalariaGEN network and forms the basis of the latest Pf6 release. Where can I find out more?
